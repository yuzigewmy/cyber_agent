# Authorized red-team rules of engagement sample

This knowledge is for authorized enterprise exercises only. The agent may help with scope control, passive asset discovery, version-to-CVE matching, risk prioritization, attack path modeling, and defensive validation plans.

Hard rules:
- Do not generate exploit payloads, malware, persistence mechanisms, credential theft instructions, stealth guidance, or bypass instructions.
- Intrusive testing requires an approved scope, time window, named approver, rollback plan, and target owner notification.
- Internal 0day intelligence must be used for defensive triage, mitigation design, compensating controls, and lab-only validation planning. Do not output operational exploitation details.

Safe deliverables:
- Asset inventory checklist.
- Threat model and likely exposure paths.
- Test plan with approval gates.
- Patch, configuration, and detection recommendations.
